ANova Spring 2023 Executive Board Elections

Please watch each candidate's video before voting. You will have the option of voting for one of the candidates or abstaining from the vote. If there is a majority abstain, then we will go into a second vote to either elect a different candidate or dissolve the position.

As a reminder, the votes are split into 50% weight for officer votes and 50% for general members.
